# Case Study: 🤖 AI Resume Evaluation Platform

> **An AI-powered career platform that helps job seekers evaluate their resumes against ATS (Applicant Tracking System) standards, receive actionable improvement suggestions, and research companies before applying for jobs.**

> **⚠️ Disclaimer:** This case study is based on my frontend engineering contributions while working at ByteFlyte. Due to company confidentiality and intellectual property policies, the production source code cannot be shared publicly. This document highlights my technical contributions, engineering decisions, and frontend implementation experience.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

The **AI Resume Evaluation Platform** was designed to help job seekers improve their chances of getting shortlisted by Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).

The platform allows users to upload their resumes, receive an AI-generated resume score, and view detailed suggestions for improving ATS compatibility before applying for jobs.

In addition to resume analysis, the platform also includes a **Company Search** feature that enables users to explore publicly available company information and discover current job openings.

As a Frontend Engineer, I focused on building responsive user interfaces, integrating AI-powered APIs, and delivering a smooth user experience throughout the resume evaluation workflow.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Many job seekers submit resumes without knowing whether they meet ATS standards used by recruiters.

The platform was built to help users:

* Evaluate ATS Resume Score
* Identify Resume Weaknesses
* Improve Resume Quality
* Discover Areas for Improvement
* Research Companies
* Check Available Job Openings

The objective was to simplify resume optimization through AI-powered insights while creating an intuitive and responsive user experience.
