# 🚀 EventBeep Recruiter Platform

> **A modern SaaS recruitment platform that streamlines hiring, recruiter collaboration, candidate management, and subscription-based recruitment services.**

> **⚠️ Disclaimer:** This case study is based on my frontend engineering contributions while working at EventBeep. Due to company confidentiality and intellectual property policies, the production source code cannot be shared publicly. This document highlights my technical contributions, engineering decisions, and implementation experience.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

The **EventBeep Recruiter Platform** is a SaaS-based hiring dashboard that enables companies to post jobs, manage applicants, collaborate with recruiters, and streamline the recruitment process from a single platform.

When I joined EventBeep, the platform was already functional but lacked a modern user experience. Many screens were not responsive, layouts were inconsistent, and several business features required frontend implementation.

My primary responsibility was to redesign and enhance the frontend while developing new features that significantly improved the recruiter experience across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Recruiters needed a centralized platform to efficiently manage their complete hiring workflow, including:

* Company Verification
* Job Posting & Management
* Applicant Tracking
* Recruiter Collaboration
* Subscription Management
* Blog & Event Publishing

The challenge was to build an intuitive, responsive, and scalable frontend capable of supporting multiple recruiter workflows while maintaining a consistent user experience across devices.
