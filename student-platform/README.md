# 🎓 EventBeep Student Career Platform

> **A modern career platform that helps students discover job opportunities, build professional profiles, apply for jobs, enroll in courses, participate in events, and accelerate their career journey.**

> **⚠️ Disclaimer:** This case study is based on my frontend engineering contributions while working at EventBeep. Due to company confidentiality and intellectual property policies, the production source code cannot be shared publicly. This document focuses on my technical contributions, engineering decisions, and frontend implementation experience.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

The **EventBeep Student Career Platform** is a comprehensive career portal designed to help students discover job opportunities, apply for positions, build professional profiles, enroll in career-oriented courses, participate in webinars and events, and stay updated through industry-focused blogs.

As a Frontend Engineer, my primary responsibility was to enhance the student experience by developing responsive user interfaces, integrating backend APIs, and implementing multiple student-facing workflows that delivered a seamless experience across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Students often rely on multiple platforms for job searching, learning, profile building, and career development.

The goal of EventBeep was to provide a single platform where students could:

* Discover Job Opportunities
* Build Professional Profiles
* Upload Resumes
* Apply to Companies
* Track Job Applications
* Purchase Career Courses
* Register for Webinars & Events
* Read Career Blogs

The platform needed to provide an intuitive, responsive, and user-friendly experience regardless of screen size or device.
